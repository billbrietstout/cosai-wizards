AI POLICY PYRAMID

From law to artefact, anchored on the CoSAI SRF

A five-tier policy pyramid for AI governance: statutes and regulations on top, engineering artefacts on the ground, with the CoSAI Shared Responsibility Framework sitting as the anchor in the middle. Six worked AI use cases (generative chat, agentic workflows, classical ML, RAG, foundation-model providers, regulated-industry deployment) are traced top to bottom with real citations.

Source alignment. Tier 3 (anchor) is the CoSAI SRF five-layer model (AI Business & Usage → AI Information → AI Application → AI Platform → AI Model Provider, §A.3). Tiers 1–2 cite statutes and standards (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, sectoral regulations). Tiers 4–5 cite threat catalogs (OWASP LLM Top 10, MITRE ATLAS, CSA AICM) and engineering-artefact templates (NIST model-card and dataset-card literature, CycloneDX ML-BOM, FDA GMLP). Single-file, dependency-free, no tracking, offered as a donation candidate under Apache-2.0.

Click any node to see its authoritative source and which use cases touch it. The navy-bordered middle tier (CoSAI SRF) is the anchor: laws and standards flow down into it; threat catalogs and engineering artefacts flow out of it.

Pick an AI use case to see its trace through all five tiers. Each tier lists the specific laws, standards, SRF layers, catalog entries, or artefacts that apply with a brief note on why.
