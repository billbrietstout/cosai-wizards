Enterprise security × CoSAI five-layer Shared Responsibility

A crosswalk between the seven classic enterprise-security domains (business, people, data, applications, infrastructure, network, physical) and the five layers of the CoSAI Shared Responsibility Framework. Each cell names the layer's role — Accountable, Responsible, Support, or Not Involved — for that security domain.

Source alignment. Column names and RACI terminology follow the CoSAI WS2 AI Shared Responsibility Framework verbatim: AI Business & Usage → AI Information → AI Application → AI Platform → AI Model Provider (SRF §A.3) with Accountable · Responsible · Support · Not Involved per §A.1 tables. The five-layer model extends the classic three-layer cloud SRM by adding L1 AI Business & Usage for sector-specific regulatory accountability (SR 11-7, FDA AI/ML, EU AI Act) and L5 AI Model Provider for training-data governance and model-artifact security. Single-file, dependency-free, no-tracking, offered as a donation candidate under Apache-2.0.
